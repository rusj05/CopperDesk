Currency Notes:

  • USD: Base currency for copper pricing.
  • EUR: Stable and widely used in international trade.
  • DKK/SEK/NOK: Nordic currencies, smaller spread but can be sensitive to energy prices.
  • JPY: Low interest rates, useful in carry trades, can be volatile during crises.
  • GBP: Stable but influenced by UK-specific events (e.g. Brexit, BoE policy).
  • CAD: Often correlated with commodity prices, especially oil.
