Trade Tips

• Check breakeven FX rate to avoid losses.
• Use your bank's FX rate for accuracy.
• Account for freight, insurance, duties.
• Payment terms impact capital and risk.

Glossary

  • Margin: Profit as % of total cost.
  • LC: Letter of Credit = bank payment guarantee.
  • FX Rate: Exchange rate used in currency conversion.
  • Breakeven: Point where profit is zero.
